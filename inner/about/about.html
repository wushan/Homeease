THE ONE-STOP GLUE GUN SHOP.

A quality product is a reflection of the entire team of specialists in each department, the commitment to excellence and the production facility itself.

Homeease’s factory is equipped with automated machines, ensuring product stability followed by prompt delivery. With Homeease’s experienced management and expert production teams, it has become the one-stop glue gun source that it is today.

Innovation is Homeease’s core business for which it has been rewarded with multiple world-wide Patents and International certificates that include TUV/GS & CE, UL, and CSA.

DESIGN, FUNCTION & APPLICATION.

For more than 30 years now, Homeease has been developing hot melt applicators and hot melt glue guns. Homeease Glue Guns can be divided into two mainstream segments; DIY (Handy Series) and professional (Heavy Series), a term mostly associated with frequent use for work-related applications.

By producing all of its products at its own facility in Southern Taiwan, Homeease is able to guarantee the overall quality, from design to the finished product. Each product is manufactured with the sole purpose of distinguishing itself from competition through design, function and application.
